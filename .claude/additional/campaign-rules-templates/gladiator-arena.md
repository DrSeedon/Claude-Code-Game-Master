## id
gladiator-arena

## name
Gladiator Arena: Rise or Die

## description
PUBG meets D&D: start as a Level 1 slave with nothing but fists. Fight monsters, level up, buy gear, climb arena ranks from Meat to God of the Arena. Permadeath. PvP tournaments. Multiple arena maps with unique hazards.

## genres
arena, gladiator, battle-royale, pvp, progression, combat-focused, dark-fantasy

## recommended_for
Campaigns focused on pure combat progression, creative tactical fights, and the thrill of permadeath. No quests, no politics — just blood, sand, and glory. Solo or competitive multiplayer.

## rules

### Core Loop
Kill → XP → Level Up → Buy Gear → Next Rank → Harder Monsters → Bigger Rewards → Repeat.
Death = permadeath. New character from scratch.

### Arena Ranks

| Rank | Hero Level | Opponent CR | Reward per fight | Unlocks |
|------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|---------|
| **Meat** | 1-2 | 1/4 - 1 | 10-50 gp | Basic shop |
| **Fighter** | 3-4 | 2-3 | 50-200 gp | Team fights, betting |
| **Gladiator** | 5-7 | 4-6 | 200-500 gp | Arena selection, sponsors |
| **Champion** | 8-10 | 7-10 | 500-2000 gp | PvP tournament, black market |
| **Legend** | 11-14 | 11-15 | 2000-5000 gp | Freedom, estate, custom arena |
| **God of the Arena** | 15-20 | 16-25+ | Priceless | Everything |

Rank-up requires winning 3 consecutive fights at current rank OR 5 total wins.
Rank-down on 2 consecutive losses (minimum: Meat).

### Starting Conditions
- Level 1, any race, any class
- No equipment. Fists only (1 + STR mod bludgeoning)
- No gold, no armor, no spells (casters have cantrips only, no material components)
- First win awards: 1 basic weapon OR 1 basic armor (player's choice)

### Fight Modes

**Solo Duel** — 1v1 against a monster or NPC gladiator. Standard mode.

**Wave Survival** — endless waves of increasing CR. Reward per wave cleared. No rest between waves. How far can you go?
- Wave 1-3: CR = rank minimum
- Wave 4-6: CR = rank maximum
- Wave 7+: CR = rank maximum + 1 per wave. Good luck.

**Battle Royale** — 4 gladiators enter, 1 leaves. Free-for-all. DM controls 3 NPC gladiators with unique builds. Winner gets all 4 entry fees + bonus loot.

**Team Fight (2v2, 3v3)** — hire NPC allies from the Barracks. They take a cut of the reward (30-50%). They can die permanently too.

**Boss Raid** — 4 gladiators vs 1 mega-boss (CR = party level + 5). Reward split equally. TPK = all dead.

**PvP Tournament** — bracket of 8 NPC gladiators + the player. Single elimination. Each NPC has a unique class/build/strategy. Winner takes the pot.

### Arena Maps

Each arena has unique terrain and hazards. DM rolls d8 or player chooses (if Gladiator rank+).

| # | Arena | Terrain | Hazard |
|---|-------|---------|--------|
| 1 | **The Pit** | 30ft mud circle, no cover | Darkness — torches are the only light, can be extinguished |
| 2 | **Sand Colosseum** | 60ft open arena, 4 pillars | Floor traps: d6 at initiative 20 — random 10ft square opens (DC 14 DEX or 2d6 fall + spikes) |
| 3 | **Flooded Ruins** | Waist-deep water, broken walls | Heavy armor = disadvantage on DEX saves. Submerged creatures lurk below (DC 14 Perception or surprise grapple) |
| 4 | **Lava Bridges** | 3 narrow bridges (5ft wide) over lava | Falling = 10d10 fire damage. Bridges crumble: each round 10% chance a random bridge section collapses |
| 5 | **Shadow Forest** | Trees, bushes, 60ft visibility | Monsters spawn from shadows every 3 rounds (CR = current rank minimum) |
| 6 | **Ice Dome** | Frozen floor, icicle pillars | Difficult terrain everywhere. DC 12 DEX on dash/charge or prone. Blizzard every 1d4 rounds: visibility 10ft, 1d4 cold/round |
| 7 | **Necropolis** | Tombstones, crypts, altars | Every creature that dies rises as a zombie (half HP, same attacks) at initiative 0 next round |
| 8 | **Shifting Maze** | 10ft corridors, walls rearrange | Walls shift at initiative 20: d4 random walls move. DC 14 INT to navigate. Dead ends = ambush chance |

### Between Fights

**Rest:** Short rest between fights (free). Long rest costs 10 gp (inn). No long rest = no HP/spell recovery beyond hit dice.

**Shop (by rank):**

| Rank | Available |
|------|-----------|
| Meat | Simple weapons, leather armor, basic supplies, torches, rope |
| Fighter | Martial weapons, medium armor, healing potions (50 gp), antitoxin |
| Gladiator | +1 weapons (500 gp), heavy armor, greater healing potions (150 gp), spell scrolls (L1-2) |
| Champion | +2 weapons (2000 gp), +1 armor (1500 gp), superior healing potions (500 gp), spell scrolls (L3-4) |
| Legend | +3 weapons (10000 gp), +2 armor (6000 gp), supreme healing potions (2000 gp), rare magic items |
| God of the Arena | Legendary items (DM discretion), artifacts, anything money can buy |

**Black Market** (Champion+ only, DC 13 Investigation to find):
- Poison (100 gp): +1d6 poison damage for 1 fight, DC 12 or target poisoned
- Cursed weapons: powerful but with drawbacks (DM generates)
- Stolen spell scrolls: 50% off, 10% chance it's fake (fizzles on use)
- Intel on next opponent: learn their stats, resistances, and strategy
- Bribe the arena master: choose your opponent (500 gp)

**Betting:**
- Bet on yourself before a fight. Minimum 10 gp, maximum = your total gold.
- Win = bet × 2. Lose = lose bet (and probably your life).
- Bet against yourself: ×3 payout, but must convincingly lose without dying. DC 15 Deception or the crowd notices and you're branded a cheater (-1 rank).

**Training:**
- Between fights, pick ONE:
  - Spar: +1 to hit on your first attack next fight
  - Study opponent: learn one resistance or immunity of next opponent
  - Rest and meditate: recover 1 extra hit die
  - Craft: DC 14 to create basic item from arena salvage

**Barracks (Fighter+ only):**
Hire NPC allies for team fights.
- Meatshield (CR 1, 25 gp): cannon fodder, will flee at half HP
- Veteran (CR 3, 100 gp): reliable, follows orders
- Elite (CR 5, 500 gp): skilled, has class features
- Champion (CR 8, 2000 gp): powerful, has their own agenda

NPCs take their cut BEFORE you split rewards. They can die permanently.

### XP and Leveling

Standard D&D 5e XP table. XP earned = monster CR reward (solo) or split (team).

**Bonus XP:**
- Flawless victory (no damage taken): +50% XP
- Killing blow was creative (DM discretion): +25% XP
- Crowd is entertained (performance/style): +25% XP
- Underdog bonus (fighting above your rank CR): +100% XP

**Level-up:** happens between fights, not during. Choose new features, spells, ability score improvements as normal D&D 5e.

### Crowd Favor

The crowd is a resource. Crowd Favor: 0 to 100. Starts at 0.

**Gaining favor:**
- Dramatic kill: +10
- Taking a hit and fighting on: +5
- Taunting the opponent: +5 (Performance DC 12)
- Using environment creatively: +10
- Winning against higher CR: +15
- Showing mercy (spare a sentient foe): +5 or -10 (crowd is fickle, DM rolls d6: 1-3 boo, 4-6 cheer)

**Losing favor:**
- Boring fight (ended in 1 round with no flair): -10
- Running away / hiding: -15
- Losing: -20
- Cheating (caught): -50

**Favor thresholds:**
| Favor | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| 25+ | Shop prices -10% |
| 50+ | Sponsor appears — free healing potion before each fight |
| 75+ | Crowd throws items mid-fight (DM rolls: weapon, potion, or rotten tomato) |
| 100 | **Emperor's Mercy**: once per arena career, if you drop to 0 HP, crowd demands you live. You stabilize at 1 HP and the fight ends in a draw. |

### Permadeath and Legacy

**Death is final.** No resurrection, no divine intervention, no take-backs.

**Legacy system:**
When your gladiator dies, your next character inherits:
- 10% of the dead gladiator's gold (hidden stash)
- Reputation echo: start with Crowd Favor = dead gladiator's Favor / 4
- One item from the dead gladiator's inventory (if someone retrieves it from the arena)

### Victory Conditions

**God of the Arena:** reach Level 20 and defeat the Final Boss — an Ancient Red Dragon (CR 24) in a solo duel. You win. Credits roll. Legend status.

**Freedom:** at Legend rank, you can choose to leave the arena. Campaign ends. Your gladiator lives on as an NPC legend.

**Dynasty:** accumulate 100,000 gp across all characters (including dead ones). Buy the arena. You are now the arena master. Campaign ends with you on top.

### Optional: Mutation System
Every 5th fight, the arena's magical wards mutate you. Roll d8:

| Roll | Mutation | Effect |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | Third Eye | Darkvision 60ft, -1 CHA |
| 2 | Thick Hide | +1 AC, -5 Speed |
| 3 | Long Arms | Melee reach +5ft, -1 DEX |
| 4 | Venomous | Unarmed attacks +1d4 poison, -1 CON |
| 5 | Regeneration | Heal 1 HP/round, vulnerable to fire |
| 6 | Berserker Blood | +2 damage when below half HP, DC 12 WIS or attack nearest creature |
| 7 | Phase Shift | Once per fight: teleport 15ft as bonus action. After use: disadvantage on next attack |
| 8 | Absorb | Resistance to last damage type received this fight. Vulnerability to opposite element |

Mutations stack. Maximum 4 mutations. Fifth mutation replaces oldest one.
